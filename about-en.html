    Faq's

    General frequently asked questions

    Find answers to common rental FAQs here. Our goal is to provide clear and concise answers for a smooth rental experience.

    The minimum age requirement to rent a car is typically 18 years old. However, the deposit required depends on the status of the driving licence of the client. If the client has a temporary driver's license, the security deposit is 1000 euros. If the client has a permanent driver's license, then the security deposit is 300 euros.

    To rent a car, please ensure you have your Driver's License and a government-issued identification document. International renters should also provide a valid passport and an international driving permit. For detailed information, kindly review our rental requirements.

    Insurance is not included in the rental car price, but we offer this option for an additional fee. Why is it beneficial? In case of emergency, with the purchased insurance, the client's liability will be €300, whereas without it, the liability is €2500.

    In case you wish to cancel or modify your reservation, please contact us immediately via phone, email, or contact form.
